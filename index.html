Breach Detection

Notifcation of confirmed breaches from darknet data and access brokers


We consist of an experienced team of cyber security professionals with decades of experience in threat actor tracking, offensive security and detection of malicious threats. We use a range of in-house developed tools and techniques to track and detect breaches and data leaks, along with emulating the tools and techniques of malicious actors who attack your network daily.

We provide security assesments and breach identitifcation via real-time monitoring of darknet data and access brokers, along with a continous moninotirng service where we alert you to discovered leaks/breaches.

Our presence in cybercrime forums allows us to passively collect data offered for sale, and developed personas enable us to privately receive data offers and identify companies who have been breached.

Our researchers identify data for sale utilizing multiple intelligence techniques, aided by inhouse tooling designed to scan and track attacker infrastucture and resouces This unique approach positions us advantageously, enabling us to capitalize on the dwell time between the initial compromise and the subsequent sale of an organization's compromised data to other malicious actors.
